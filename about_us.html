About Us

Our Expertise

This is what we do

Digital Studio offers custom software development and IT outsourcing services. Our developers create customized software for individuals, start-ups and small and medium-sized businesses.

Since our founding in 2018, we have grown, increasing productivity and expanding our customer list. We have implemented more than 20 completed projects, which means that more than 15 customers have chosen us. These were different orders for small and large projects, and we successfully completed all tasks.

Based on years of experience, we know that each business has a different software and hardware environment. That is why we provide a wide range of software development services, as well as to meet the needs and requirements of customers for the most modern technologies.

Founder and CEO - Jose Dominguez

Jose has engineered & deployed data intelligence and data integration systems to drive growth at companies and organization in financial services, non-profit services, public schools and higher education, insurance agencies and multi-national organizations.

Jose Dominguez is a graduate of Rutgers University and since college has had a love for computer science, data engineering, predictive analytics and helping businesses use their data to drive growth and create operational efficiencies.
